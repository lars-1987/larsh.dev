Privacy

Last updated: 1 July 2026

This is my personal site, run by Lars Holmström in Melbourne, Australia. The short version: no accounts, no cookies, and nothing that identifies you personally. I use privacy-respecting analytics to understand how the site is used, and I honour Do Not Track.

01

The short version

  • No accounts, no sign-up, and no email needed to browse.
  • No cookies are set on your device.
  • I use privacy-respecting product analytics (PostHog), including session replay, to see how the site is actually used.
  • If your browser sends a Do Not Track or Global Privacy Control signal, none of it loads at all.
  • I never sell your data, and there is no advertising.
02

What is collected

When Do Not Track is not set, the site loads PostHog, which records:

  • Usage analytics — which pages and features you use, clicks, scrolls and navigation, the site that referred you, and general device information (browser and operating system type, screen size, language).
  • Session replay — a reconstruction of how you interacted with a page: a synthetic playback of what you clicked, where you moved, and what changed on screen, so I can see how the site is used and improve it. It is not a video or screen recording of your device, and any text typed into a field is masked by default and not captured (the site has no forms in any case).
  • Approximate location — PostHog uses your IP address to estimate a coarse location (country, sometimes region or city). It is used only in aggregate and is not used to identify you.

I do not collect names, email addresses, passwords, or payment details, because this site has no accounts and asks for none of those.

03

Cookies and on-device storage

This site is cookieless — it sets no cookies. So that a visit reads as one coherent session across pages, PostHog stores a single random, anonymous session identifier in your browser's local storage. It is not linked to your identity, it is not a cookie, and you can remove it at any time by clearing this site's data in your browser.

04

Do Not Track

I honour the Do Not Track (DNT) and Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals. If either is enabled, the analytics script loads nothing at all — no analytics, no session replay, no requests — and you browse with zero measurement.

05

Where your data goes

Analytics are processed by PostHog Inc. on its United States cloud, acting as my data processor. If you visit from outside the US (including Australia, the EU and the UK), anonymous usage and replay data is transferred to and processed in the United States.

The site itself is served as static files by my hosting provider, which may keep standard server logs (such as IP address and browser type) for security and reliability. There are no other third-party services, trackers, or advertising networks.

06

How long it is kept

Usage and session-replay data are retained by PostHog under my account's retention settings and then deleted. Because nothing is linked to an identifiable person, individual records cannot be tied back to you.

07

Your rights

Depending on where you live — for example under the Australian Privacy Act, the EU/UK GDPR, or the California CCPA — you have rights over personal data about you, such as access, correction, and deletion. Because this site is anonymous and cookieless, I usually hold no information that can be connected to you individually. If you have a question or request, email me and I will help where I can.

08

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13, and I do not knowingly collect information from them.

09

Changes

If this policy changes, I will update this page and the date above.
