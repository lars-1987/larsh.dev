Questions, answered.

Do I need to book in advance?
Booking ahead guarantees your spot and the fastest turnaround, but you're welcome to drop in during opening hours for a quick assessment. Busy weeks fill up, so online booking is the safest bet.
Which brands and groupsets do you service?
All of them. We're Shimano and SRAM specialists and service everything from entry-level commuters to high-end road, gravel and mountain bikes: mechanical and electronic (Di2 / AXS) included.
How long will my service take?
Most standard services are completed within 48 hours. Wheel builds and bike fits are booked by appointment. If a job needs ordered-in parts we'll let you know the timeline upfront.
Will you contact me before doing extra work?
Always. You'll get a fixed quote after inspection and we never carry out work you haven't approved. No surprises on the invoice, ever.
Do you offer a warranty on your work?
Yes. All labour is covered by a 30-day workmanship guarantee, and fitted parts carry their manufacturer's warranty. If something isn't right, bring it back and we'll sort it.
How do I pay, and can I get a quote first?
We accept card, tap-to-pay and bank transfer. Every job over a basic tune starts with a free quote after inspection, so you'll know the cost before we begin.
