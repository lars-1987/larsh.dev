Questions. Answered.

How much is my property worth?
Value comes down to your home's position, presentation and the current market, not a single online estimate. We provide a considered, evidence-based appraisal drawn from comparable local sales and live buyer demand, with no obligation.
How long does a campaign take?
A typical campaign runs three to four weeks from launch to sale, plus a short preparation period beforehand. We'll recommend the timing and method, whether auction, private sale or expressions of interest, that best suits your home.
What are your fees?
Our fee is a transparent percentage of the sale price, agreed in writing upfront with no hidden costs. Marketing is quoted separately and tailored to your campaign, so you only invest in what will move the result.
When should I sell?
The best time depends on your circumstances and the market, not just the calendar. Spring and autumn are traditionally strong, but well-presented homes sell well year-round. We're happy to advise honestly on timing at your appraisal.
