Questions. Answered.

How much do veneers cost?
Veneers are tailored to each patient, so the investment depends on the number of teeth and the materials used. We'll give you a clear, fixed quote at your consultation, with no obligation to proceed.
Is Invisalign painful?
Most patients describe mild pressure for a day or two when starting a new set of aligners, rather than pain. It settles quickly, and because the aligners are smooth and removable, day-to-day life carries on as normal.
How long does treatment take?
It varies by treatment: whitening and bonding are often a single visit, veneers usually take two to three appointments, and Invisalign typically runs from six to eighteen months. We'll map your timeline at the planning stage.
Can I finance my treatment?
Yes. We offer interest-free payment plans on most cosmetic treatments so you can spread the cost comfortably. We're happy to walk through the options at your consultation.
Which treatment is right for me?
That's exactly what your consultation is for. We assess your smile, listen to your goals, and use digital planning to show you the options, so the decision is informed and entirely yours.
