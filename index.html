SanctumPDF

Convert PDFs and financial files without uploading a thing.

Wasm does the heavy lifting in the browser; the server only handles auth, payments and licence checks. Files live in memory and vanish when the tab closes. Fourteen locales took the indexed surface from 36 pages to more than 335.

MetaStrip

Metadata removal that never touches a server.

Hand-written MP4, ID3, Vorbis and RIFF parsers swap metadata for identical-length padding, so playback never breaks. Zero upload, zero server, zero cache: the guarantee is the architecture, and you can verify it in the Network tab.

Renew

Skincare tracking with real analytics, from camera to correlation.

Point the camera at a product: OCR lifts the label and Gemini turns it into structured, searchable ingredient data, translating Korean and Japanese along the way. Logging is offline-first and instant, then syncs to Supabase as the cross-device source of truth.
